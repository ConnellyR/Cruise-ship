Long Beach

Long Beach is a coastal city and port in Southern California. Some activities in Long Beach California are going to Knotts Berry Farm. Newport

Newport Oregon is a beach city with many interesting activities. Near the beach there is an aquarium, and a sea loin sanctuary. On the same street there is many things to do loike going glass blowing. They also have a huge candy store with for those who have a sweet tooth. They also have one of the national Ripleys Beleive it or Not Museums.

Anchorage

Anchorage Alaska. There is plenty to keep you busy in Anchorge. There is a booming downtown that has many things to do. Besides that there is an extreme amount of nature to be seen. The windy beaches, the frosty forest, the artic glaciers. There are countless tourist attractions like the zoo, also that glacier tour is nothing to sneeze at. Doesnt matter what time of year it is there is always something for you to do in Alaska

Seattle

Seattle Washington. There is always something to do in Seattle Washington. You can visit the space needle. You can spend a day downtown. Even an afternoon at one of the countless stunning parks. You can catch a Seattle Mairiners game. There is always something to do here. Have fun!
